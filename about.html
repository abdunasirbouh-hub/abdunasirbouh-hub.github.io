About AgrilinkEthiopia

Empowering Ethiopian farmers and revolutionizing agricultural trade through innovative digital solutions

Founded in 2024, AgrilinkEthiopia was born from a simple observation: Ethiopian farmers produce exceptional agricultural products, but struggle to connect with the right buyers at fair prices. We saw an opportunity to bridge this gap using technology.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of farmers and buyers across Ethiopia, creating a more transparent, efficient, and equitable agricultural marketplace.

Our Mission

To empower Ethiopian farmers by providing them with direct access to markets, fair pricing, and the tools they need to grow their businesses sustainably.

We believe that by connecting farmers directly with buyers, we can:

  • Eliminate unnecessary middlemen
  • Ensure fair prices for farmers
  • Provide fresh, quality products to buyers
  • Promote sustainable agricultural practices
  • Strengthen Ethiopia's agricultural economy

Our Vision


To become Ethiopia's leading agricultural marketplace, transforming how farmers and buyers connect, and setting new standards for transparency, efficiency, and fairness in agricultural trade.

We envision a future where every Ethiopian farmer has access to digital tools, market information, and direct buyer relationships that enable them to thrive and contribute to national food security.
