Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a farmer on AgrilinkEthiopia?

Click on the "Register" button in the navigation menu, select "Farmer" as your account type, and fill in your details. You'll need to provide your farm information, location, and identification documents for verification.

How long does it take to get verified as a seller?

Our verification process typically takes 2-3 business days. We'll review your documents and contact you if we need any additional information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, mobile money (M-Birr, HelloCash, CBE Birr), and cash on delivery for verified transactions.

Do you provide delivery services?

Yes, we can help coordinate delivery within Addis Ababa and major cities. For bulk orders, we work with logistics partners to ensure safe delivery nationwide.

How do I report a problem with my order?

You can contact our support team via email at support@agrilinkethiopia.et or call our hotline. We aim to resolve all issues within 24 hours.
