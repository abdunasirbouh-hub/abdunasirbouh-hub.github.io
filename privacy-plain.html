Privacy Policy - AgrilinkEthiopia

Last Updated: November 30, 2025

At AgrilinkEthiopia, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you register and use AgrilinkEthiopia, we collect:

Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, password

Profile Information: Business name, location, profile picture

Farmer-Specific Data: Farm location, types of products, farming certifications

Buyer-Specific Data: Business type, delivery addresses

Product Listings: Product descriptions, photos, pricing, inventory

Transaction Data: Purchase history, payment information, delivery details

Communications: Messages between users, support inquiries

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

Device Information: IP address, browser type, device type

Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns

Location Data: Approximate geographic location (if you enable location services)

Cookies: Small data files stored on your device for functionality and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Provide and maintain our platform services

Process transactions and facilitate connections between farmers and buyers

Verify farmer accounts and product listings

Communicate with you about your account, orders, and platform updates

Improve our services and develop new features

Prevent fraud and ensure platform security

Comply with legal obligations

Send marketing communications (with your consent)

Analyze platform usage and user behavior

Resolve disputes and provide customer support

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

When you create a listing or make a purchase:

Farmers' business names, locations, and product details are visible to buyers

Buyers' names and delivery addresses are shared with farmers for order fulfillment

Contact information is shared after a transaction is initiated

3.2 With Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third parties who help us operate our platform:

Payment processors (for secure transaction processing)

Cloud hosting providers (for data storage)

Analytics providers (to understand platform usage)

Communication services (for sending emails and notifications)

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

Comply with legal processes or government requests

Enforce our Terms of Service

Protect the rights, property, or safety of AgrilinkEthiopia, our users, or others

Prevent fraud or security threats

3.4 Business Transfers

If AgrilinkEthiopia is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

Encryption of data in transit and at rest

Secure server infrastructure

Regular security audits and updates

Restricted access to personal data

Employee training on data protection

Important: While we take security seriously, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

5.1 Access and Update

You have the right to:

Access your personal information

Download a copy of your data

5.2 Delete Your Account

You can request account deletion at any time. Note that some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes.

5.3 Marketing Communications

Contacting us directly at privacy@agrilinkethiopia.et

5.4 Cookies

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

Provide our services

Comply with legal obligations

Enforce our agreements

After account deletion, we may retain certain information for legitimate business purposes or as required by law.

AgrilinkEthiopia is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Ethiopia. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

11.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies: Required for the platform to function properly, including login authentication and security features.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform, including page views, traffic sources, and user behavior patterns.

Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings to provide a personalized experience.

Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track campaign effectiveness (only with your consent).

11.2 Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit our platform, you can choose to:

Accept All: Allow all cookies including analytics and marketing cookies

Necessary Only: Only essential cookies required for platform functionality

Reject All: Decline all non-essential cookies (may limit platform features)

You can change your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings or by contacting us.
